In this Blocksworld game, you start with 15 blocks arranged as follows: Block 5, Block 2, Block 12, Block 1, Block 14, Block 3, Block 11, Block 7, Block 10, Block 9, and Block 6 are all on the table. Block 15 is stacked on top of Block 2, with Block 4 on top of Block 15. Additionally, Block 13 is on Block 1, and Block 8 is on Block 13. Your goal is to rearrange these blocks so that Block 7 and Block 8 are on the table, with Block 5 on Block 8. Block 15 should be on Block 5, followed by Block 6 on Block 15, and Block 4 on Block 6. Block 11 should sit on top of Block 4, with Block 10 on Block 11, and Block 3 on Block 10. Block 14 should be on Block 3. Meanwhile, Block 13 needs to be on the table with Block 9 on top of it, followed by Block 12 on Block 9. Finally, Block 1 should be on top of Block 12, and Block 2 should be on Block 1. Currently, your arm is empty, ready to start the rearrangement.
