In the Blocksworld game, you have 7 blocks arranged in a specific configuration. Initially, Block 5, Block 3, Block 2, and Block 6 are all on the table. Block 1 is on top of Block 6, while Block 4 is also on the table with Block 7 on top of it. Your arm is currently empty. The goal is to rearrange the blocks so that Block 1, Block 4, and Block 5 are on the table. Block 6 should be placed on Block 4, and Block 7 should be on top of Block 6. Meanwhile, Block 3 should be moved onto Block 5, with Block 2 remaining on the table.
