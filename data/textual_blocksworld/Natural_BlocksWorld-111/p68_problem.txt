In the Blocksworld game, you start with 10 blocks arranged in the following order: Block 2, Block 6, Block 9, Block 7, Block 5, Block 1, Block 10, Block 8, Block 3, and Block 4 are all on the table. Your arm is currently empty. Your goal is to rearrange the blocks so that Block 8 is on the table with Block 7 on top of it, Block 9 is on the table with Block 10 on top of it, Block 3 on top of Block 10, Block 5 on top of Block 3, and Block 2 on top of Block 5. Block 4, Block 6, and Block 1 should remain on the table.
