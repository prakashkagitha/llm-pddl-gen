In the Blocksworld game, you have 13 blocks that need to be rearranged. Initially, the blocks are in the following positions: Block 11, Block 5, Block 6, Block 12, and Block 3 are all on the table. Block 10 is stacked on Block 12, with Block 7 on top of Block 10, and Block 13 on top of Block 7. Block 9 sits on Block 3. Block 1, Block 8, Block 2, and Block 4 are also on the table. You need to use your empty arm to move the blocks to achieve the following configuration: Block 13 should be on the table with Block 3 on top of it, followed by Block 2, then Block 4, and finally Block 12 on top. Block 11 should remain on the table, with Block 6 on top of it, followed by Block 7, and then Block 8. Block 10 should be on the table with Block 1 on top of it, followed by Block 5, and finally Block 9 on top.
