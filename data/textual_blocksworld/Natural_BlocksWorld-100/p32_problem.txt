In the Blocksworld game, you have a set of 15 blocks arranged in a certain configuration. Currently, the blocks are arranged as follows: Block 10, Block 2, Block 11, Block 14, Block 1, Block 8, Block 12, Block 13, Block 5, Block 4, and Block 15 are all on the table. On top of Block 14 is Block 7; on top of Block 13 is Block 6; on top of Block 5 is Block 9; and on top of Block 15 is Block 3. Your arm is empty, and your goal is to rearrange the blocks into a new configuration where Block 8, Block 4, Block 10, Block 7, Block 6, Block 1, Block 14, Block 3, Block 15, Block 9, Block 5, Block 2, and Block 12 are on the table. Additionally, Block 11 needs to be placed on top of Block 15, and Block 13 needs to be placed on top of Block 5.
