In the Blocksworld game, you have 9 blocks initially arranged as follows: Block 5 is on the table, Block 8 is on the table with Block 2 on top of it, Block 4 is on the table, Block 7 is on the table, and Block 3 is on the table with Block 1 on top of it and Block 6 on top of Block 1. Additionally, Block 9 is sitting on the table. Your arm is currently empty. Your objective is to rearrange the blocks into the following configuration: Block 6 should be directly on the table with Block 1 on top of it, Block 4 on top of Block 1, and Block 9 on top of Block 4. Block 2 should be on the table with Block 5 on top of it, Block 7 on top of Block 5, Block 3 should be on the table, and Block 8 should be on top of Block 3.
