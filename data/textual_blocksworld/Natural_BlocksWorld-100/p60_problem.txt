In the Blocksworld game, you have a configuration with 9 blocks arranged as follows: Block 2, Block 9, Block 4, Block 1, Block 7, Block 8, and Block 3 are all on the table. Block 5 is placed on top of Block 8, and Block 6 is sitting on Block 3. Your arm is currently empty. The goal is to rearrange the blocks into a new configuration where Block 2, Block 3, Block 5, Block 1, Block 6, and Block 8 are all on the table. Meanwhile, Block 7 should be placed on top of Block 6, Block 9 should be on the table with Block 4 stacked on top of it.
