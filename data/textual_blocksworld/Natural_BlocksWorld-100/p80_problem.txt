In the Blocksworld game, you start with 7 blocks arranged as follows: Block 5 is resting on the table, and Block 4 is stacked on top of Block 5. Block 1 is perched on Block 4, with Block 2 sitting on top of Block 1. Separately, Block 6 is on the table, and Block 7 is on Block 6, with Block 3 on top of Block 7. Currently, your arm is empty. Your objective is to rearrange the blocks into a new configuration: Place Block 3 directly on the table. Stack Block 6 on Block 3. Place Block 2 directly on the table, then put Block 5 on Block 2. Stack Block 1 on Block 5, and place Block 7 on Block 1. Finally, finish by placing Block 4 on Block 7.
